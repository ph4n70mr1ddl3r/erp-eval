## W120. Internal Audit Planning & Risk Assessment

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Annual planning cycle |
| **Frequency** | Annual (with quarterly updates) |
| **Volume** | ~30–40 high-priority audits planned per year |
| **Owner** | Head of Internal Audit |
| **Participants** | Audit Team, Board Audit Committee, CEO, CFO |

### Steps

| # | Activity | Role (R) | Role (A) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Gather data on risk indicators: shrinkage (W92), margin exceptions (W85), high turnover | Audit Team | Head of IA | 2 weeks |
| 2 | Survey department heads on process risks and control weaknesses | Audit Team | Head of IA | 1 week |
| 3 | Rank entities/processes by risk score (Impact x Likelihood) | Head of IA | Head of IA | 3 days |
| 4 | Develop Annual Audit Plan (list of stores, DCs, and processes to be audited) | Head of IA | CEO | 3 days |
| 5 | Present plan to Board Audit Committee for final approval | Head of IA | Audit Committee | 2 hours |
| 6 | Allocate audit staff and timeline in ERP/Audit Management System | Audit Team | Head of IA | 1 day |

## W121. Operational Audit Execution (Store/DC/HQ)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Approved Audit Plan (W120) or special request from CEO |
| **Frequency** | Monthly (rotational) |
| **Volume** | ~3–4 audits executed per month |
| **Owner** | Senior Internal Auditor |
| **Participants** | Audit Team, Store/DC Manager (Auditee) |

### Steps

| # | Activity | Role (R) | Role (A) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Announcement of audit (or unannounced for cash/inventory checks) | Audit Team | Head of IA | — |
| 2 | Fieldwork: Test controls for POS (W5), Inventory (W4), Petty Cash (W25), LP (W37) | Audit Team | Senior Auditor | 3–5 days |
| 3 | Identify discrepancies: Missing documents, unauthorized overrides, stock variance | Audit Team | Senior Auditor | Ongoing |
| 4 | Exit Meeting: Discuss preliminary findings with Auditee Manager | Senior Auditor | Senior Auditor | 2 hours |
| 5 | Draft Audit Report: Categorize findings (Critical, Major, Minor) | Audit Team | Senior Auditor | 3 days |
| 6 | Management Response: Auditee provides Corrective Action Plan (CAP) | Auditee | Auditee | 1 week |
| 7 | Final Report Distribution & Filing in ERP | Head of IA | Audit Committee | 1 day |

## W122. Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Review

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Quarterly risk committee meeting |
| **Frequency** | Quarterly |
| **Volume** | Top 20 corporate risks reviewed |
| **Owner** | Head of Internal Audit (acting as Risk Officer) |
| **Participants** | Executive Team (C-Level), Board of Directors |

### Steps

| # | Activity | Role (R) | Role (A) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Update Enterprise Risk Register: Financial, Operational, Strategic, Compliance | Head of IA | CEO | 1 week |
| 2 | Assess external risks: Inflation, port congestion, regulatory changes, competition | Executive Team | CEO | Ongoing |
| 3 | Review mitigation plans for top risks (e.g., FX Hedging W80, DR Plan W55) | Head of IA | CFO/CIO | 2 days |
| 4 | Present Risk Dashboard to the Board | Head of IA | CEO | 2 hours |

## W123. Fraud Investigation Protocol

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Whistleblower tip (W79), LP Exception (W37), or Audit discovery |
| **Frequency** | Ad-hoc |
| **Volume** | ~5–10 investigations/year |
| **Owner** | Head of Internal Audit |
| **Participants** | HR (for employee discipline), Legal (for prosecution), Security |

### Steps

| # | Activity | Role (R) | Role (A) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Intake of report/discovery; initial evidence review (CCTV, ERP logs) | Audit Team | Head of IA | 1–2 days |
| 2 | Form Investigation Committee (IA, HR, Legal) | Head of IA | CEO | 1 day |
| 3 | Detailed Forensic Audit: Transaction tracing, witness interviews | Audit Team | Head of IA | 1–3 weeks |
| 4 | Investigative Report: Document findings, suspects, and loss value | Head of IA | CEO | 3 days |
| 5 | Administrative Hearing / Disciplinary Action | HR Manager | CHRO | 1–2 weeks |
| 6 | Legal Action (if applicable): Filing of criminal/civil cases | Legal Counsel | VP Legal | 1–3 months |
| 7 | Control Gap Remediation: Update process to prevent recurrence | Head of IA | CEO | 1 week |

---

### System Touchpoints (Audit)
- Audit log tracking (ERP activity logs, POS override logs)
- Exception reporting dashboard (Shrinkage, Voids, High-value returns)
- Audit Management portal for report storage and CAP tracking
- Risk Register module for ERM tracking
- Access control reviews (SOD - Segregation of Duties matrix)

## W159. Anti-Bribery & Corruption (ABC) Monitoring & Audit

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Periodic audit cycle; or "Red Flag" system alert |
| **Frequency** | Quarterly monitoring; Annual audit |
| **Volume** | Covers high-risk areas (Procurement, Permits, Real Estate) |
| **Owner** | Compliance Officer |
| **Participants** | Internal Audit, Procurement, Legal |

### Steps

| # | Activity | Role (R) | Role (A) | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Red Flag Monitoring**: Monitor high-risk transactions: non-PO payments to "consultants," gifts/entertainment expenses, and unusual vendor credit notes | Compliance Officer | Head of IA | Weekly |
| 2 | **Vendor Due Diligence**: Perform background checks on vendors in high-risk categories (Construction, Government Sales) | Procurement | Compliance Officer | 1 day/vendor |
| 3 | **Conflict of Interest (COI)**: Annual collection of COI disclosures from all employees in Procurement and Management | HR | Legal | 2 weeks |
| 4 | **Audit**: Detailed audit of LGU permit payments (W54) and land acquisition fees to ensure compliance with the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and local anti-graft laws | Internal Audit | VP Legal | 1 week |
| 5 | **Training**: Mandatory ABC training for all employees with spending authority | HR / Compliance | CHRO | Annual |
