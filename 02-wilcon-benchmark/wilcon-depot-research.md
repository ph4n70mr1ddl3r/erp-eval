## 11. Lessons from Wilcon for ERP Design

### What Wilcon Does Well (to emulate)
1. **Omnichannel maturity**: Seamless online-offline integration
2. **Category management**: Deep expertise in tiles, plumbing, building materials
3. **Store format flexibility**: Different formats for different markets
4. **Supplier partnerships**: Direct imports, exclusive arrangements
5. **Customer experience**: In-store consultation, project advice, installation services
6. **Digital content**: How-to content drives both foot traffic and online engagement

### What an ERP Must Handle for This Type of Business
1. **Massive SKU counts** (20,000–60,000+)
2. **Complex product attributes** (tiles: size, material, finish, color; lumber: species, dimensions)
3. **Import management** (LC tracking, customs, landed cost calculation)
4. **Multi-format retail** (potentially different price lists/inventory per format)
5. **Heavy DC-to-store replenishment** (high frequency, complex routing)
6. **BOPIS orchestration** (order routing, store-level fulfillment, pickup confirmation)
7. **Project-based selling** (B2B customers buying for construction projects)
8. **Catch-weight / variable measurement items**
9. **Consignment inventory management**
10. **Philippine tax and regulatory compliance**
