Wilcon Depot — Real-World Benchmark Research

Wilcon Depot (PSE: WLCON) is the largest home improvement and construction supply retail
chain in the Philippines. This document compiles publicly available information about Wilcon
to serve as a real-world reference for our model company ("BuildRight Depot Corp.").

11. Lessons from Wilcon for ERP Design

What Wilcon Does Well (to emulate)

  1. Omnichannel maturity: Seamless online-offline integration
  1. Category management: Deep expertise in tiles, plumbing, building materials
  1. Store format flexibility: Different formats for different markets
  1. Supplier partnerships: Direct imports, exclusive arrangements
  1. Customer experience: In-store consultation, project advice, installation services
  1. Digital content: How-to content drives both foot traffic and online engagement

What an ERP Must Handle for This Type of Business

  1. Massive SKU counts (20,000–60,000+)
  1. Complex product attributes (tiles: size, material, finish, color; lumber: species, dimensions)
  1. Import management (LC tracking, customs, landed cost calculation)
  1. Multi-format retail (potentially different price lists/inventory per format)
  1. Heavy DC-to-store replenishment (high frequency, complex routing)
  1. BOPIS orchestration (order routing, store-level fulfillment, pickup confirmation)
  1. Project-based selling (B2B customers buying for construction projects)
  1. Catch-weight / variable measurement items
  1. Consignment inventory management
  1. Philippine tax and regulatory compliance
